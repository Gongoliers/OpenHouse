Beta Test Open House

Team 5112, The Gongoliers

What is it?
This year, our team participated in the FRC 2016 beta test. We tested the new Java WPILib software and roboRIO images with our robot and verified that it was bug free and tried a variety of new features. Your team is welcome to join us at our open house in which we will demo the software for you and let you know about any important changes. We will also walk you through our example programs that we created. Note: we did not test the new hardware for the 2016 season.

When is it?
The beta test open house will be on December 31st at 8:45 - 9:50 AM.

Where is it?
The open house will be at Ponaganset High School: 137 Anan Wade Rd, North Scituate, RI 02857 in room 326.
